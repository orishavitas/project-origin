## Primary Competitors

### Bouncepad
- **Website:** https://www.bouncepad.com
- **Headquarters:** London, UK (global distribution)
- **Focus:** Premium tablet enclosures for enterprise
- **Product Categories:** Floor stands, wall mounts, desk stands, kiosks
- **Target Verticals:** Retail, hospitality, healthcare, corporate
- **Search Terms:** "bouncepad [category]", "bouncepad [device]"
- **Strengths:** Brand recognition, design quality, healthcare presence
- **Typical Price Range:** $300-$700

### Displays2Go
- **Website:** https://www.displays2go.com
- **Headquarters:** Fall River, MA, USA
- **Focus:** Value-oriented display solutions
- **Product Categories:** Tablet stands, kiosks, digital signage mounts
- **Target Verticals:** Retail, trade shows, hospitality
- **Search Terms:** "displays2go tablet", "displays2go kiosk"
- **Strengths:** Price competitiveness, wide selection
- **Typical Price Range:** $100-$400
